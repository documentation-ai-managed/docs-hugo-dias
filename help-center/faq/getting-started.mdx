## Quick Start

To create your first documentation project in Hugo Dias:

1. Log in to your Hugo Dias dashboard.
2. Click **"New Project"**.
3. Enter your project name and description.
4. Click **"Create"**.
5. Begin adding pages and organizing your docs.

Your project is live! Edit, update, and share docs anytime from the dashboard.

```tip
You can invite collaborators from the project settings page.
```
