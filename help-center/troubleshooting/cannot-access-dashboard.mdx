---
title: Cannot Access Dashboard
description: Troubleshoot access issues in Hugo Dias.
---

## Symptoms

- Seeing a blank page or login loop
- Receiving an access denied error

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your internet connection">
    Ensure your device is online and not behind a restricted VPN.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear your browser cache">
    Try reloading after clearing cookies and cache.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    Use the "Forgot password" link at login if needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If the issue continues, contact support at support@hugodias.com.
